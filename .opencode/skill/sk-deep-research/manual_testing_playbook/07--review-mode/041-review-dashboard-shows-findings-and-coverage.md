# DR-041 -- Review dashboard shows findings and coverage

This document captures the realistic user-testing contract, current behavior, execution flow, source anchors, and metadata for `DR-041`.

---

## 1. OVERVIEW

This scenario validates the review dashboard output for `DR-041`. The objective is to verify that the review dashboard includes Findings Summary (P0/P1/P2), Progress Table with dimensions, Coverage (files/dimensions/traceability protocols), and Trend.

### WHY THIS MATTERS

The dashboard is the operator's live view into review progress. Without a clear findings summary by severity, dimension progress tracking, and coverage metrics, operators cannot tell whether the review is progressing meaningfully or stalling — and they cannot spot coverage gaps until the final report, when it is too late to extend the loop.
