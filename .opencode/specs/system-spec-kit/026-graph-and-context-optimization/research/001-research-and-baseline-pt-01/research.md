## 3. Methodology
The audit used a bounded five-iteration deep-research loop executed through Copilot CLI on `gpt-5.4` with `reasoningEffort=high`, under the skill-owned workflow for this packet. Each pass had a single Q-lane focus: iteration 1 built the complete gap inventory, iteration 2 audited convergence quality, iteration 3 firmed severity and enumerated blind spots, iteration 4 pressure-tested recommendation survival under temporal drift and blind spots, and iteration 5 ranked the cheapest next actions.
