## [**1.2.2.0**] - 2026-02-04

Introduced 6 Coding Analysis Lenses (CLARITY, SYSTEMS, BIAS, SUSTAINABILITY, VALUE, SCOPE) and 6 Anti-Patterns. Removed Narsil tool connection in favor of built-in exact text search/file-pattern search/Read tools. The main changes here are coding lenses and anti-patterns. The goal is to make the workflow easier to follow and easier to trust.
