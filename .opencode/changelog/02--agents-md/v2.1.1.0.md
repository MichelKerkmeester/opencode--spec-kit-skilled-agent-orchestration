### Category: Adversarial Self-Check Protocols

- **@review: Adversarial Self-Check (R1-R5)** — New Section 9.1 with 3-pass Hunter/Skeptic/Referee protocol for P0/P1 findings. Adds sycophancy warning, summary table template, Fast Path skip. Self-validation expanded to 6 questions. New anti-pattern: "Never let sycophancy bias findings"
