## [**2.1.1.0**] - 2026-03-05

Added Hunter/Skeptic/Referee adversarial methodology to @review, @debug, and @ultra-think agents across all 5 runtime directories (several files). Fixed pre-existing Gemini tool name inconsistencies in the related file and the related file. The main changes here are @review: adversarial self-check and @debug: adversarial hypothesis validation.

---

### @review: Adversarial Self-check

- Need: People needed clearer support for this new part of the workflow.
- What changed: New Section 9.1 with 3-pass Hunter/Skeptic/Referee protocol for P0/P1 findings. Adds sycophancy warning, summary table template, Fast Path skip. Self-validation expanded to 6 questions. New anti-pattern: "Never let sycophancy bias findings".
- Why it matters: This makes the workflow easier to discover and use.

---

### @debug: Adversarial Hypothesis Validation

- Need: People needed clearer support for this new part of the workflow.
- What changed: New Phase 3.1 between Hypothesize and Fix with Counter-Evidence Search, Alternative Explanation Check, Anchoring Check, Prior Attempt Echo Check, and Post-Challenge Re-Ranking Table. New anti-pattern: "Never skip adversarial validation".
- Why it matters: This makes the workflow easier to discover and use.

---

### @ultra-think: Adversarial Cross-critique

- Need: People needed clearer support for this new part of the workflow.
- What changed: New Section 6.1 with cross-strategy Hunter/Skeptic/Referee challenge (triggered when strategies within 15 points). Convergence Check flags artificial agreement. New anti-pattern: "Convergence Sycophancy".
- Why it matters: This makes the workflow easier to discover and use.
